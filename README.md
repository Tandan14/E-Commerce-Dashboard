# E-Commerce-Dashboard

This repository contains an interactive "E-Commerce Sales Dashboard" built using "Power BI".   The project analyzes key business metrics such as "Total Revenue, Total Unit Sold, Total Ad Cost Per Click , Total Clicks and Category Performance to help online retail businesses make smarter, data-driven decisions.

**Project Overview** 
The E-Commerce Dashboard provides a complete view of business performance by combining KPIS and visuals. 

It helps dtakeholders quickly understand

Which products and categories drive the most revenue
How sales change over time
Who the top category is 
Which regions perform best
Profitability across categories and segments

**Key Features**
Total Revenue

Total Units Sold

Total Impressions

Total Ad_Cost Per Click

Total Clicks

Total Ad_Spend

**Tools & Technologies**
Excel Data Cleaning & preparation

Power Query- Data transformation

Power BI - Dashboard development

DAX - Calculations and measures
